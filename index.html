Introduction

Master of arts at Institute of Applied Arts, National Chiao Tung University, Taiwan, researching Human-Computer Interaction. I worked with professors Robin Bing-Yu Chen, I-Ping Chen, Da-Yuan Huang, and Gary Hsieh in HCI lab, National Taiwan University.

Prior to my graduate studies, I attended the Computer Science Department in National Taiwan University of Science and Technology for my undergraduate degree. As an undergraduate I worked with professor Da-Yuan Huang to conduct HCI experiments and research in haptic feedback in VR, professor Wen-Kai Tai to learn game design skills and theory.
